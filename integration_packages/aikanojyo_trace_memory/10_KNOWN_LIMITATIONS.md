# Known Limitations

- Generalization performance drops.
- Not a general Japanese segmenter.
- Participant Reference does not cover all expressions.
- Negation, correction, and tense are incomplete.
- Mixed Script Promotion has limited dataset-level gain and is default OFF.
- Terminal Boundary candidates are noisy and diagnostic only.
- Word-only traces can reconstruct meaning incorrectly.
- Not proven better than existing Embedding/AIKanojyo Memory.
- Not validated in AIKanojyo.
- 500T-style fixtures are synthetic.
- Real lover-style conversations are unproven.
